About Us

This team competes in the FTC Robotics Competition each school year. We compete against schools from other towns within Massachusetts. Each year brings us a new challenge and our team is constantly changing and adapting.

Our team consists of the most intelligent students in the school, and in our efforts we approach every challenge with a keen mind and passionate spirit. We utilize programming, science, engineering, robotics (of coooourse), and a vast knowledge of technology and the world around us to hone our skills and win competitions. It is our hope that you support our club in any way possible, and take note, WE ARE ALWAYS LOOKING FOR NEW MEMBERS!


Can I Join Robot Club?

We ABSOLUTELY take anybody who's willing to join and learn how to make a robot. Only requirements we have are: alive, human, in grades 9-12, and somehow present at Milton High School in Milton, MA, USA. We officially meet on Tuesdays and Thursdays, although we'd like to meet everyday when possible. We'll say when we meet on extra days.

Take a look at the Members page in the main menu above for more information!

If you already know how to make a robot then it would be great if you could teach us something too.

3D Printing skills may be used and/or taught in this club. Robot building and programming will definitely be taught to any and all members of the club.

Can I talk to the Robot Club?

We'd love to talk about robots with anybody, even if you're not in high school! You can send email or hangouts messages to us at mhsrobotclub@gmail.com. You can text or call us at (617) 898-0775
